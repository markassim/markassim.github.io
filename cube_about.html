Cube Game

The process of how cube game was made.

Concept

To start working on the project, a design challenge was made to be a tower defense game. To make the tower defense game unique, I decided to make the playing field on the surface of a cube. Then, during the remaining first week, the concept was further developed, revolving around the cube world idea and by the end of the week, the concept was pitched.

Design

After pitching the concept, there was useful feedback on how the concept could be further improved, so during the design stage, I decided to make a diagram of the mechanics and their interactions between eachother so its clear how the design go further.

Playtesting

During the final week of the development, there was a playtest event to validate if the design works as intended and so after the event, I applied some quick fixes regarding feedback, such as centering on the main base when starting the game.

Wrap-up

As a result, the game is playable, unfortunately, is missing visual feedback and polishing.
